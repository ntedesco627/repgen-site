Privacy Policy

Your data, your workout.

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your name, email, and username. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive an Apple-issued identifier instead of your email. Workouts, history, schedules, and preferences you create in the app are stored so the app can sync across sessions. If you enable notifications, we store a push token to send reminders.

How we use it

We use your data to run the app — syncing workouts, generating AI plans, and sending notifications. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Third-party services

We use third-party providers for hosting, authentication (Apple Sign-In), and push notifications. These services operate under their own privacy policies and process data only as needed to deliver the app.

Workout sharing between users

RepGen allows you to share workouts with other users in the app. When you share a workout, the recipient receives a copy of the workout content you created (name, exercises, sets, and reps). Only the specific user you choose to share with can see this content — shared workouts are not posted publicly or visible to other users. Your account name or username may be visible to the recipient as the source of the shared workout. You are in control of what you share, and you can delete workouts you have created at any time.

Security & retention

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We retain your data indefinitely until you request deletion — your workouts, history, and account information remain available to you for as long as you use the app. To have your account and all associated data permanently deleted, contact us at support@repgen.fit and we will process your request promptly.

Your rights

You can view and update your profile inside the app. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. California residents (CCPA) and users in the European Economic Area (GDPR) have additional rights, including the right to data portability and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact support@repgen.fit.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after updates constitutes acceptance.
