  • Themes & notifications – Light/dark mode and optional reminders.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

RepGen (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is a workout tracking and planning app. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account data: When you register, we collect email, name, and username (and optional Apple Sign-In identifier if you use Sign in with Apple).
  • Workout data: Workouts you create, schedules, and history (sets, reps, duration) are stored to provide the service.
  • Device data: If you enable notifications, we store a push token to send you reminders.
  • Preferences: Theme and notification settings are stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Data

We use your data to provide the app (sync, AI planning, notifications), improve the service, and communicate with you (e.g. support replies). We do not sell your personal data.

4. Third Parties

We use services for hosting, authentication (e.g. Apple Sign-In), and push notifications. These providers process data according to their privacy policies and our instructions.

5. Security & Retention

We use HTTPS and secure practices. We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Your Rights

You can access and update profile information in the app. To delete your account or request a copy of your data, contact us at support@repgen.app.

7. Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date at the top will change, and we will post the new version here.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@repgen.app.
